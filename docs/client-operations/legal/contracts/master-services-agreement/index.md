# Master Services Agreement

**Document:** Master Services Agreement  
**Version:** 1.0  
**Owner:** Metaphor Automation Consulting  
**Status:** Client-facing draft for counsel review  
**Classification:** Confidential business agreement

The MSA establishes the baseline commercial terms for consulting, software engineering, AI systems, workflow automation, websites, applications, maintenance, and related professional services. It governs Statements of Work and addresses scope control, payment, acceptance, intellectual property, AI use, confidentiality, data protection, warranties, indemnification, liability, termination, dispute resolution, and general terms.

The source document expressly states that it should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before use.
