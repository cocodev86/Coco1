# Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement

**Document:** Mutual Non-Disclosure Agreement  
**Version:** NDA v1.0  
**Owner:** Metaphor Automation Consulting  
**Status:** Draft for legal review  
**Classification:** Confidential

The Mutual NDA protects nonpublic business, financial, customer, software, AI, technical, security, product, operational, and personal information exchanged while evaluating or performing Metaphor services. It includes exclusions, reciprocal confidentiality duties, residual-knowledge and anonymized-insight provisions, AI and data-handling restrictions, required disclosure, ownership, return or destruction, remedies, Georgia governing law, and Cobb County venue.
